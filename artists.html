KAWS

Brian Donnelly, a.k.a. KAWS, is a New York based artist who is famous for designing limited edition toys and clothing. Born in 1974 in Jersey City, New Jersey, KAWS graduated from the School of Visual Fine Arts with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in illustration. In 1996, Donnelly worked at Disney where he contributed in works such as 101 Dalmations, Daria, and Doug. He then had the opportunity to design limited edition vinyl toys which were a big success in Japan. Recently, he has done collaborations with Nike, Vans, and Hennessy.

David Choe

David Choe Was born on April 21, 1976 and spent his childhood in Koreatown, Los Angeles. He is an American figure painter, muralist, graffiti artist and graphic novelist of Korean descent. His paintings illustrate a theme of desire, degradation, and exaltation, with a raw tone, that he himself has named “dirty style”. In 2007, Facebook’s Mark Zuckerberg hired Choe to paint murals for their office in exchange for stock shares which ended up being worth $200 million in 2012.
